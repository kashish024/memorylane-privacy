Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a child profile?

Tap the "ADD" button in the header on the home screen, then enter your child's name, birthdate, and select an avatar.

How do I add a memory?

Tap "Add Memory" from the home screen or the + button in the bottom navigation. Choose photo, camera, or voice to capture your memory.

Can I backdate memories?

Yes! When creating a memory, tap "Backdate this memory" to select any date in the past.

How do monthly email summaries work?

Navigate to the Monthly Summary screen from your home screen. The AI will generate a beautiful summary of your child's memories for the current month and send it to your email.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All data is securely stored in Firebase with enterprise-grade encryption. You can export your data at any time from Settings.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Export Memories. You'll receive a JSON file with all your memories that you can download and save.
