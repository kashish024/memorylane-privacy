MemoryLane: Family Journal

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Summary: MemoryLane stores your family memories securely in the cloud. We use your data only to provide the app's features (like AI summaries and monthly emails). We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MemoryLane: Family Journal ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using MemoryLane, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account creation date

2.2 User Content

You create and upload:

  • Child profile information (name, date of birth, emoji/photo avatar)
  • Memory text, titles, and dates
  • Photos (stored in Firebase Cloud Storage)
  • Voice recordings (stored in Firebase Cloud Storage)
  • Milestone tags and categories

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage statistics (features used, session duration)
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Subscription status and billing information (through Apple/Google)

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts from your phone
  • Data from other apps
  • Biometric data
  • Payment card information (handled by Apple/Google)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 To Provide the Service

  • Store and display your family memories
  • Process photos and voice recordings
  • Generate AI-powered monthly summaries using your memory text
  • Send monthly summary emails to your registered email
  • Enable sharing with family contributors
  • Create automatic data backups (Premium feature)

3.2 To Improve the Service

  • Analyze app usage to fix bugs and improve features
  • Monitor performance and uptime
  • Understand which features are most popular

3.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send monthly summary emails
  • Send service announcements (new features, maintenance)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send subscription-related notifications

4. Third-Party Services

MemoryLane uses the following third-party services to operate:

4.1 Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)

What it does: Authentication, database, and file storage

Data shared: All user content, account info

Location: US data centers

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 OpenAI

What it does: Generates AI summaries and transcribes voice notes

Data shared: Memory text for summaries, audio files for transcription

Data retention: OpenAI does not use your data to train their models (per enterprise agreement)

Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

4.3 Resend

What it does: Sends monthly summary emails

Data shared: Your email address and summary content

Privacy Policy: https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

4.4 RevenueCat

What it does: Manages subscriptions and purchases

Data shared: User ID, subscription status, purchase receipts

Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • All data is stored on Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) in US data centers
  • Photos and audio files are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Database records are stored in Cloud Firestore

5.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Data at rest is encrypted in Firebase storage
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Access controls limit who can view your data
  • Regular security audits and updates

5.3 Security Limitations

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your password confidential
  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Logging out on shared devices
  • Reporting suspicious activity immediately

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 We DO Share Data With:

  • Contributors: People you invite can view and add memories for specific children
  • Service Providers: Firebase, OpenAI, Resend, RevenueCat (as described above)
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect safety

6.2 We DO NOT Share Data With:

  • Advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Marketing companies
  • Social media platforms
  • Any third party for commercial purposes
