# MemoryLane Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 3, 2025**

## Introduction

MemoryLane ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address for account creation and login
- **Child Profiles**: Names, birthdates, and avatar selections for your children
- **Memory Content**: Text, photos, and voice recordings you create
- **Milestone Information**: Tags and categories you assign to memories

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App interactions and feature usage
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the MemoryLane service
- Enable AI-powered features (voice transcription and monthly summaries)
- Send monthly email summaries (if you opt in)
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Infrastructure
- All user data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
- Photos and audio files stored in Firebase Storage
- Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest

### Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption
- Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
- Regular security updates and monitoring

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### OpenAI
- **Purpose**: Voice transcription (Whisper API) and AI-generated monthly summaries (GPT-4)
- **Data Shared**: Voice recordings and memory text for processing
- **Privacy Policy**: https://openai.com/privacy

### Resend
- **Purpose**: Email delivery for monthly summaries
- **Data Shared**: Your email address and generated summary content
- **Privacy Policy**: https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

### Firebase/Google Cloud
- **Purpose**: Data storage, authentication, and hosting
- **Data Shared**: All app data
- **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your stored data within the app
- **Export**: Download your complete data as a JSON file
- **Delete**: Remove your account and all associated data at any time
- **Opt-Out**: Disable monthly email summaries in app settings

## Children's Privacy

MemoryLane is designed for parents to document their children's memories. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All data is entered and managed by parent/guardian users.

## Data Retention

- Memory data is retained until you delete it or close your account
- Account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure
- Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via:
- In-app notification
- Email to registered address
- Updated "Last updated" date on this page
